Disaster Reporting System

Households that have been affected by natural disaster in the United States can use this Django application to receive a quick prediction of the amount of federal aid they should expect to receive. Built using data from OpenFEMA, families will no longer have to wait several months to know about their federal financial aid.

Snackr

As many people fall short of having healthy diets, this Laravel application recommends healthy food items from the USDA Food Composition Database to people based on their dietary history, allergies, and recommended daily values. Users can also keep track of their weekly nutritional intake with charts.

It Takes a Village

Games are often only thought of as entertaining, but they can be therapeutic too. This game, built in Unity3D in collaboration with psychology researchers, strives to relieve symptoms of parents suffering with postpartum depression.

Vascularized Bone Tissue Engineering

For patients who need new limbs, bone tissue engineering provides long-lasting solutions that seek to mimic natural bone as much as possible. The growth of blood vessels in bone is especially difficult to produce, but this project strives to do so using growth factor microspheres in 3D cell culture.

Mobile Phone Radiation

While we can't imagine ourselves without our mobile phones nowadays, the health side effects of mobile phone radiation has always been a controversial issue. This project investigates the effect of mobile phone radiation on our skin, the outermost part of our body.
